Set up the right tent

A camping trip with the children, an expedition to the North Pole or a music festival experience at Rock Werchter with your friends? Take the test and determine which tent best fits your activity.

  • Ultra light/mountains

  • i

    • I’m looking for challenging adventures through mountain and valley

      A tent from our Performance category is probably the best choice for you: suited for almost every season, limited in space, light and compact to carry. Ideal for long-distance journeys and challenges at a certain height.

  • Trekking Backpack
    •
  • i

    • I like to travel on foot with my backpack

      You are a true explorer. Your ideal tent is small- to medium-sized and resistant to all types of weather. As you are exploring the world on foot, the weight and packing volume of your tent is very important to you. You should take a medium to large budget into account.

  • Family camping
  • i

    • A pleasant camping trip with the whole family!

      You are looking for a spacious tent from our Comfort camping category. Its weight is of no importance to you. Your budget varies from affordable to luxurious. Comfortable camping tents are especially suited for camping during the summer months.

  • Fun camping
    •
  • i

    • I only sleep in a tent when I visit a music festival in summer

      Our Fun camping category is probably the best fit for you. These tents are only suitable for use in summer. The weight of the tent is of lesser importance. You expect your tent to be an affordable ‘mobile house’ which is not too big.
